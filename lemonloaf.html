My Lemon Loaf Recipe

Baking has always been a hobby of mine and my favorite thing to make is lemon loaf, so I wanted to share it.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon zest (about 2 lemons)
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Grease and line a loaf pan.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In different bowl, mix eggs and sugar until fluffy. Add melted butter, Greek yoghurt, lemon zest, lemon juice and mix until smooth.Then mix everything together.
  4. Pour into loaf pan and bake for 50 minutes.
