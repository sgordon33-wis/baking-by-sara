About Me and My Recipes

This page shares the story behind my favorite lemon loaf and why I love baking it! My name is Sara, and one of my hobbies is baking. However, I dislike complicated recipes, as they often don't turn out right. So, I started focusing on simpler recipes that still provide me with baked goods that bring me a lot of joy. Lemon loaf is my favorite because it is easy to make, and the acidity and sweetness are a perfect combination, in my opinion. I will leave a link below where you can view my recipe. Enjoy!
